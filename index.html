OUR VISION

Within our lifetime, all individuals around the world will have access to a widely recognized and accepted international digital financial identity and financial health score as well as the information, resources, tools and support necessary to be active participants and creators in the world economy.

.

In the United States, there are 106 million people
who are underbanked or unbanked.

Traditional financial institutions don't not work for millions of people in the U.S. and around the world.

Credit scores don't work across borders.

"The underbanked are people or businesses that have poor access to mainstream financial services normally offered by retail banks. The underbanked can be characterized by a strong reliance on non-traditional forms of finance and micro-finance often associated with disadvantaged and the poor, such as cheque cashers, loan sharks and pawnbrokers. Many people who are classified as Underbanked may also refer to those who have language barrier such as migrant workers, unable to access to banking facilities due to distance such as the elderly people, or simply feel uncomfortable using Automated Teller Machines." - Wikipedia

KONVIV is here to change that.

A globally accesible financial identity for everyone on the planet

Konviv is a mobile startup that helps individuals create a digital financial identity while implementing a new way to budget, track, manage and grow wealth by providing the necessary financial literacy, tools, capital resources, and support to help you reach financial freedom. Konviv users will be able to receive incremental loans through our peer-to-peer marketplace based on their Konviv score and get help paying it back through AI-driven notifications and "smart buckets" that automatically adjust your spending and savings. As you grow, Konviv uses machine learning to understand your financial behavior and provides you customized financial guidance.

We are currently building an Android, iOS version, and SMS analog version of our application.

Konviv is a data-driven mobile platform that provides smart budgeting tools, financial literacy, peer-2-peer lending, and personal support for the underbanked.


Our Three Pillars of Success


GLOBAL FINANCIAL IDENTITY AND SCORE

Through the continued use of Konviv, individuals will create and inform their Konviv score, an alternative credit score. This will serve to establish a global digital financial identity that will allow users to access increasing financial opportunities all over the world, including credit.

DATA-DRIVEN "BUCKETS": TRACK YOUR SPENDING

Konviv will allow users to budget, track, manage and grow their wealth. Through our platform, individuals and businesses will be able to track and trade money in multiple currencies, they can track commodities (raw goods), and better manage many other types of assets.

PEER-2-PEER LENDING: ACCESS TO CAPITAL

Konviv users will be able to receive incremental loans through our peer-to-peer marketplace based on their Konviv score. The P2P marketplace allows individuals on our platform to lend each other small amounts of money, encouraging investing and savings.

Mobile financial solutions are gaining traction around the world.

In Kenya, more than 72% of people living on less than $1.25 a day are now using M-Pesa, a mobile money system.

Today, more than 43% of Kenya's GDP flows through M-Pesa with over 237 million peer-to-peer transactions.

First Nations Communities Pilot

Konviv has the privilege to work with various First Nations on a pilot involving various communities, including the Apache, Seneca, and Lakota. Tribal leaders from these three communities have given Konviv their go ahead to launch the product in these communities. We are honored and humbled.

The Konviv pilot will serve to create a financial platform that community members can use to engage in trade, manage their assets, and gain access to capital that has long been denied due to many individuals not having access to or wanting traditional credit.

The meaning of KONVIV

Konviv comes from the Spanish word: convivir. Convivir is a beautiful, poetic, and lively word. It literally means to coexist or to exist side by side with something. Convivir acknowledges that we are all here together and part of the same story and trajectory. It urges us to think of the world, our actions, and our work as more than our own, it is ours.

Konviv is an acknowledgement that finances are an undeniable part of our social fabric. With every financial transaction there is a human exchange. Konviv strives to make each financial exchange more touching, more empowering, and more impactful. Konviv - more than a financial app - is a movement.
